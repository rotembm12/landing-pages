About Us

The Beating Heart of Israel's Diamond Trade

The Diamond Exchange Trading Floor in Ramat Gan is one of the world's most important diamond trading centers, offering a rare window into the craft, commerce, and culture behind Israel's billion-dollar diamond industry. Whether you're a seasoned dealer, collector, or a curious visitor, our trading halls bring together polished and rough diamonds alongside cutting-edge gemological technology.

With two dedicated trading halls, an advanced technology wing, and a rich legacy spanning decades, we invite you to experience the brilliance that has made the Israel Diamond Exchange a global landmark ranked among the top attractions in Ramat Gan.
