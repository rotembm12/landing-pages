Who We Are

Six Decades of
Building Israel

Since 1963, Hanson Israel has been the trusted partner behind the nation's most ambitious construction projects — from residential towers and commercial developments to critical infrastructure that shapes communities.

As a proud subsidiary of the Heidelberg Materials Group, one of the world's largest integrated building materials companies, we combine global engineering standards with deep local expertise. Our Hadera factory is one of 25 concrete plants operating across Israel, ensuring reliable supply wherever your project demands.

We don't just deliver concrete — we deliver confidence. Every batch is precision-engineered, quality-tested, and dispatched with the logistical efficiency that keeps your project on schedule and on budget.

What We Deliver

Complete Building
Materials Solutions

From foundation to finish, we supply the materials that make construction possible.

Ready-Mix Concrete

Custom-engineered concrete mixes for every application — from high-strength structural pours to specialized self-compacting formulations, delivered fresh to your site.

Aggregates & Quarry

Crushed stone, gravel, sand, and specialty aggregates sourced from our own quarries — graded and tested to meet the most demanding structural specifications.

Asphalt Production

Hot-mix and warm-mix asphalt for roads, parking areas, and infrastructure projects — produced at our dedicated facilities for consistent quality and rapid delivery.

Cement Supply & Storage

Bulk cement via our dedicated import terminal and regional storage facilities — ensuring uninterrupted supply for large-scale and ongoing construction operations.
